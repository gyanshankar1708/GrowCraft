About Us

Who We Are — GrowCraft

GrowCraft is a service-based platform designed to help businesses grow their online presence while also empowering students and contributors to gain hands-on experience in real-world projects. We pair client work with mentorship-driven contributor flows so learning happens by doing.

This project is proudly part of GirlScript Summer of Code 2025 (GSSoC'25) — a nationwide open-source program where contributors collaborate, learn, and build impactful solutions.

Open Source & Community

Join GrowCraft — contribute, learn, and grow together

Being part of GSSoC 2025, GrowCraft is open for contributions. We welcome developers, designers, writers, and digital marketers to collaborate and build impact.

Every contribution counts — whether it’s fixing a bug, designing a page, writing content, or suggesting improvements. Start with issues labeled good-first-issue or GSSoC.

  • Follow CONTRIBUTING.md for workflow and branch rules.
  • Open a clear PR with screenshots and description.
  • Request mentor review if needed — mentors will guide you.
